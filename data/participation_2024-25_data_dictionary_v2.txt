NOTES
-----
- All 15 features have 100% non-null coverage (34,378 rows each).
- Coded missing values (-3, -4, -5, 997, 999) are present in the data and must
  be handled during preprocessing (drop, recode to "Unknown", or impute).
- CULTSATIS has ~71% routing skips (-3) — only asked to a subsample.
- CHERVIS12_NET is a behavioral variable (heritage visits), not demographic.
  Justified for policy use case (profiling under-engaged populations).
- emdidc19 is an area-level variable (Index of Multiple Deprivation), not
  individual-level. Decile 1 = most deprived, 10 = least deprived.
